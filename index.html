WHAT IS SPACE ODYESSY?

Gear up for a heart-pounding solo adventure in a desolate space station! As a lone operative, you'll take on the daunting task of purging this derelict structure of a hostile enemy infestation. Leveraging C++ and Unreal's Blueprint visual scripting, we've made our game playable directly in your web browser! Sharpen your skills, strategize your attacks, and unleash a torrent of firepower to cleanse the station and emerge victorious.

Made with Unreal Engine 5
